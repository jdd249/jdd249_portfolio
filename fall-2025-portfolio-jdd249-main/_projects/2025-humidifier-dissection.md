## Overview
I dissected and analyzed an ultrasonic humidifier on a team of 5 people. We took apart the humidifier, analyzed each of the fluid mechanical components, and compiled a 5 minute long video describing the form and funciton of each component. Throughout this process I gained hands on experience with fluid mechanical devices and improved my ability to apply fluid mechanical theory to practical devices.

## Individual Contribution

My role on the team was primarily research/analysis. More specifically, I leveraged previous fluid mechanics knowledge and online resources to calculate the forces on atomized water particles, and I calculated critical velocities in the centrifugal fan inside the humidifier. In addition to research, I also aided in the physical dissection of the humidifier and compiling/documenting our findings.
